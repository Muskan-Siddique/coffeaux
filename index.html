what makes our coffee special

specialty coffee has a rich and crispo flavour , and this comes not just from the quality of the specialty cofee plant and beans, but also from the hard work and dedication of its farmer.

farmers must take extra care to ensure that the beans are harvested at the right time to achieve maximum flavour from them.
